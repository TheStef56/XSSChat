These vulnerabilities are caused by two misconfigurations:

1:
    {{ messages|safe }}

the "|safe" flag, tells to jinja2 to avoid escaping, permitting injection

2:
    app.config['SESSION_COOKIE_HTTPONLY'] = False

this makes the cookies be accessible from javascript
